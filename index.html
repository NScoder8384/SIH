Help

This platform allows you to verify the authenticity of your academic certificates. Employers, institutions, and government agencies can check credentials here. For assistance, contact: support@satyadocs.jh.gov.in

About SATYADOCS

SATYADOCS is a digital platform by the Government of Jharkhand to verify certificates from higher education institutions. It detects fake degrees using OCR, AI, and blockchain, ensuring academic integrity and public trust. Aimed at admissions offices, employers, and government bodies for secure verification.
